Meet the Eagle

Eagles are large, powerfully-built birds of prey, with heavy heads and beaks.

Some random information

Eagles have relatively high body heat.

The bald eagle is actually a sea eagle (Haliaeetus species) that commonly occurs inland along rivers and large lakes.

Eagles can fly as high as 15,000 feet.

The sea eagles (sometimes called fish, or fishing, eagles, Haliaeetus species) are very large eagles that live along rivers, big lakes, and tidewater.
